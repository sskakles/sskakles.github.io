Hospitals & Clinics

For the past decade, I have referred my clients to St. David's Adaptive Driving Program, and in 2019, I was hired to join their team as an active employee.

St. David's Rehabilitation Hospital Adaptive Driving Program is the only one of its kind in Central Texas. Because driving is an essential part of maintaining independence, the program promotes self-reliance through medically-based driver evaluation and training. A registered occupational therapist with extensive driver rehabilitation experience and training will provide a comprehensive assessment and recommendations. If you would like to speak with me about getting started through St. David's, you can email me at melissa.bell@stdavids.com. My St. David's driving rehabilitation hours are select weekdays between 8am-5pm.

St. David's does not presently offer mobile OT driving rehabilitation services and only operates Mon-Fri, 8am-5pm during "non-inclement" weather. Clients who require extended service needs (i.e., weekends, weekdays before 8am or after 5pm, driving during "inclement" weather) can work with me privately through Safe Driving Rehab, LLC.

For questions about these private services, please email me at missy@safedrivingrehab.com or call my mobile number at 512-689-0236.
