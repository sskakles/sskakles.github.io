OT-First

The OT-FIRST framework guides the occupational therapist seeking to address young driver needs while following current best practices using evidence-based methods. Please visit the Training Page to access training or purchase the OT-FIRST manual, which includes a comprehensive driver readiness assessment tool supported by research.
